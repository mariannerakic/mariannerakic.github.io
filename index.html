About me

My life can be summarized in three cities, Liege, Zurich and Boston.

I was born and raised in Liege. After my Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering at the University of Liege, I went on pursuing a Master's degree at ETH Zurich in Electrical Engineering and Information Technology. Finally, I am finishing my Master Thesis at MIT in Boston.

I am passionate by computer vision and its different applications, in particular in healthcare. For the past months, I have been leveraging learning-based registration techniques to adapt them to new problems. I also love travelling, dansing, reading.
